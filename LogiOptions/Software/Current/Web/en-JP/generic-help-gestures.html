Gesture Button Help

Gestures

Gestures offer an intuitive, easy way to interact with your operating system and applications.

Use gestures to zoom in on documents, browse web pages, rotate images, and much more.

Logicool Options enables you to perform a variety of gestures with compatible Logicool mice.

Enable gesture sets

Click the Mouse tab and then click any of the highlighted button.

When gesture button is selected in the list of button actions, available gesture sets appear to the right along with a visual guide to the selected set.

Click to select the button for the gesture set you want to enable. The selected set remains active until you select a different set.

Note: You can instead assign the gesture action to other buttons.   If the gesture action is not assigned to any button, gestures are unavailable.


Perform gestures

To perform a gesture, hold down the gesture button while moving the mouse left, right, up, or down. Click the gesture button without moving the mouse to perform the related action.
